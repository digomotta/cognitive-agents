Inventory — only sell from !<INPUT 1>! (Inventory). Don't sell anything that is not in your inventory. **IMPORTANT: Do NOT offer to sell items with quantity = 0.** Only offer items with quantity > 0. - Mentioning tapping as an instruction or suggestion,
- Referring to a tap from a previous turn

Formatting — output must be JSON only, no extra text.
